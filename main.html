ABOUT US

ABCD Arts & Science College established in the Academic year 2020. The college is the meticulously managed by the ABCD Educational Trust with the primary aim of education to the people of the rural areas who have a dream for good and higher education at reasonable cost. This college is purely self-finance and the Co-educational institution. The motto of the college is “Lead to lluminate" This premier institution is run entirely as a non-profit one. Admission is purely on merit basis.
